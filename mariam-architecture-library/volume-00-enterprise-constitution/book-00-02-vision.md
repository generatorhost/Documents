Version: v1.0-draft

## 1. Vision Statement

Mariam AI Enterprise OS is designed to become an autonomous enterprise operating system that discovers opportunities, understands work, plans execution, coordinates swarms of agents, governs quality, delivers outputs, and evolves through DNA.

## 2. Strategic Vision

Mariam will operate as a digital enterprise capable of supporting freelance work, remote work, enterprise operations, knowledge work, marketplace activity, and future business domains through reusable AI capabilities.

## 3. Vision Pillars

1. Autonomous enterprise execution.
2. Swarm-based intelligence.
3. DNA-driven growth.
4. Governance-first operation.
5. Knowledge-centered learning.
6. Capability-based planning.
7. Multi-domain expansion.

## 4. Long-term Outcome

Mariam becomes a self-improving operating environment where every agent, workflow, capability, plugin, connector, model, policy, and department can be strengthened through approved DNA packages.

## 5. Acceptance Criteria

This book is accepted when the long-term direction, strategic pillars, and enterprise vision are clear and traceable to future roadmap and blueprint volumes.
