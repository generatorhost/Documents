Version: v1.0-draft

## 1. Purpose

This book defines the artificial intelligence principles governing Mariam AI Enterprise OS.

## 2. AI Operating Principles

1. AI work is mission-driven.
2. AI agents operate under defined roles.
3. AI agents use approved tools and providers.
4. AI outputs pass review and validation.
5. AI reasoning is captured where useful for audit and improvement.
6. AI work is governed by permissions, policy, quality, and risk controls.
7. AI capabilities are reusable across domains.
8. AI performance is measured through metrics and task outcomes.
9. AI behavior improves through approved DNA evolution.
10. AI systems are replaceable through provider abstraction.

## 3. Model Role

Models are providers inside Mariam. They support agents, tools, reasoning, generation, analysis, validation, and review.

## 4. Agent Role

Agents are enterprise workers with mission, context, tools, memory, capabilities, policy, and DNA.

## 5. Acceptance Criteria

This book is accepted when Mariam's AI principles clearly define model, agent, provider, governance, and improvement rules.
